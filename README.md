By day I'm a Real Estate Leasing & DataOps Analyst at a NYC commercial real-estate firm, where I've recovered **$700K+ in billing**, migrated **200+ entities** from SAGE to Yardi, and automated away **8+ hours of manual work a month**. By night (and in my M.Sc. Data Science at Fordham) I build the AI layer I wish that industry had: retrieval over leases, agentic violation-tracking, and quantitative models for ranking equities.

I care about the part most prototypes skip — making the thing **production-grade**: walk-forward validation instead of leaky backtests, eval harnesses for RAG, and deployments that actually stay up.

### Featured work

| Project | What it is |
|---|---|
| **[sp100-rank](https://github.com/malithjd/sp100-rank)** | Cross-sectional return ranking of S&P 100 equities. Walk-forward CV across Linear/RF/XGBoost/LightGBM, Rank IC as the primary metric, SHAP stability across folds, and IC→PnL translation. The opposite of an overfit backtest. |
| **[BringTheApp](https://github.com/malithjd/BringTheApp)** | Deployed GenAI agent that analyzes auto purchase agreements and surfaces the terms dealers bury. Live, not a notebook. |
